# Customer_Purchase_Analysis
An exploratory data analysis (EDA) of electronic sales data from September 2023 to September 2024 using Python, Pandas, Matplotlib, and Seaborn. This project uncovers key sales trends, customer behavior, product performance, and add-on purchase patterns.

# Electronic Sales Data Analysis (Sep 2023 - Sep 2024)

This Google Colab notebook presents a comprehensive exploratory data analysis (EDA) of an electronic sales dataset covering transactions from September 2023 to September 2024. The analysis aims to extract valuable insights into sales performance, customer preferences, and product dynamics to inform strategic business decisions.

## Key Objectives:
- **Data Understanding & Cleaning**: Initial assessment, handling missing values, and data type conversions.
- **Sales Performance**: Identifying monthly and yearly sales trends, including peak periods.
- **Customer Behavior**: Analyzing loyalty member activities, order statuses, and payment method preferences.
- **Product Analysis**: Determining top-rated products, understanding SKU distributions, and identifying add-on purchase patterns.
- **Financial Metrics**: Calculating key statistics like mean, median, min, and max total purchase prices.
- **Visualizations**: Creating various plots (bar charts, histograms, scatter plots) to visualize distributions and relationships.

## Technologies Used:
- Python
- Pandas (for data manipulation and analysis)
- Matplotlib (for data visualization)
- Seaborn (for enhanced data visualization)

## Key Findings & Insights:
- **Smartphone** was identified as the product type with the highest number of 5-star ratings, indicating strong customer satisfaction.
- Approximately **21.72%** of customers are Loyalty Members, with a notable **32.76%** of them having cancelled orders, highlighting an area for customer retention improvement.
- **Credit Card** and **PayPal** are the most preferred payment methods.
- **Smartphones**, **Tablets**, and **Laptops** are significant drivers for add-on sales (Accessories, Extended Warranty, Impulse Items).
- **May** recorded the highest monthly sales, and **2024** shows robust overall sales growth compared to 2023.

## Recommendations:
- Implement targeted cross-selling strategies for smartphone, tablet, and laptop customers based on add-on purchase patterns.
- Focus on understanding and reducing order cancellations among loyalty members.
- Optimize payment incentives for both cash and digital payment methods.
- Leverage monthly sales trends for strategic inventory and promotional planning.
